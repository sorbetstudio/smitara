Smitara Foundation

Smitara Foundation is a Mumbai-based non-profit organization dedicated to helping people who are fighting for their lives but don't have the money to pay for the surgeries they need.

Learn More

What We Do

  • Raise funds to cover the costs of surgeries
  • Work with hospitals and doctors to ensure the best care
  • Provide post-surgery support and follow-up care
  • Offer emotional support and counseling

Why Support Us

  • Transparency

    We are committed to being transparent and accountable in all our work.

  • Impact

    Every rupee donated goes directly towards saving lives.
